Added five new QA-build levels after Level 16.

Affected additions:
- Level 17: Bridge Arms - longer 3-slider / 3-rotator elevated route.
- Level 18: The Folding Route - longer rectangular sequence with 4 rotators and 3 lifts.
- Level 19: Counterweight Walk - higher multi-step climb with 4 sliders and 4 rotators.
- Level 20: The Inner Circuit - longer circuit path with 4 sliders and 5 rotators.
- Level 21: The Upper Mandala - longest structure with 6 sliders and 6 rotators.

Rules followed:
- No redesign.
- Existing visual style preserved.
- Existing mechanics only: walkable blocks, vertical sliders, rotators, support pillars.
- Each added level is longer and harder than the previous one.
- Slider final positions avoid direct geometry merge.
- Rotator alternate orientations are spaced with clearance.
- Handles use visible crankFace values where needed.
