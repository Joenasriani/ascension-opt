Raising/lowering block SFX patch

Changed only the slide/raising-block activation sound function.

Before:
- Square wave
- 200 Hz
- 0.2 s
- 0.03 peak gain
- Harsh mechanical tone

After:
- Softer sine low tone at 180 Hz
- Very light triangle overtone at 240 Hz
- Lower peak gains: 0.018 and 0.01
- Slightly longer decay for smoother movement feel

Gameplay, levels, music, UI, title, and assets were not changed.
